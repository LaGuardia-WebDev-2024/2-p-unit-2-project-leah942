Iceland

Things to see

Iceland is known for it's beautiful views, with lots of volcanoes and glaciers. The most popular sight is the northern lights. There are also a lot of ice caves.

Things to do

A lot of tourists enjoy bird-watching, there are a lot of rare and exclusive birds that you can find in their natural environment. There is also whale watching.

Things to eat

Icelands food is very simple. The country has access to a lot of natural resources and clean water, so their food is very fresh. They eat a lot of seafod and bread
